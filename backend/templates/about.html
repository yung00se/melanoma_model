About AI Skin Check

This tool uses deep learning to classify skin lesions as malignant or benign, helping with early detection of skin conditions. A result from a team project made by students at the University of Kansas, DermaScan AI uses classification methods to distinguish between harmful and harmless growths, lesions, marks, and other skin conditions. A user simply has to upload an close-up image of their growth and the DermaScan AI will assess how threatening it is (confidence level coming soon).
